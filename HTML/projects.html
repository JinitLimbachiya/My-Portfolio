I have developed a website called "Planetary Weight Calculator" that allows users to calculate their weight on other planets in our solar system by entering their weight on Earth. The website is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to provide a user-friendly and interactive experience.

I am thrilled to share that Dan Goldin, NASA's 9th Chief, has reacted positively to my project. His acknowledgment and support are a great honor and a testament to the project's potential and impact.

The "Planetary Weight Calculator" aims to educate and engage users by demonstrating how gravity varies across different planets. This project not only highlights the fascinating differences in planetary environments but also sparks curiosity about space exploration and science.
